About

I am a postdoctoral scholar at Vanderbilt University investigating the extremes of AGN variability as a member of the international Sloan Digital Sky Survey collaboration's Black Hole Mapper Quasar Physics Working Group, specializing in spectroscopic and timing analysis. Through mentorship and service I continue to work to improve STEM education for students of all backgrounds and abilities.
